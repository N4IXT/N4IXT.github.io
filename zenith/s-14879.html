Zenith Model S-14879

Years of Production: 1946-1948

The Zenith Model S-14879 is a classic post-war radio known for its elegant design and reliable performance. It was produced during the late 1940s, a period when Zenith was at the forefront of radio innovation.

Technical Specifications:

  • Frequency Range: AM and Shortwave bands
  • Power Supply: AC-powered with a built-in transformer
  • Speaker: 6-inch dynamic speaker for rich sound quality
  • Tubes: Utilizes 6 vacuum tubes for signal amplification
  • Cabinet: Wooden cabinet with a polished veneer finish

Notable Features:

  • Integrated antenna system for improved reception
  • Backlit tuning dial for easy operation in low light
  • High-quality craftsmanship with durable components
  • Classic Art Deco styling, making it a collector's item

Historical Context:

The Zenith Model S-14879 was introduced during the post-World War II era, a time when radio technology was rapidly advancing. It was designed to meet the growing demand for high-quality home entertainment. Zenith's reputation for innovation and reliability made this model a popular choice among consumers.

Today, the Zenith Model S-14879 is highly sought after by vintage radio enthusiasts and collectors. Its combination of technical excellence and timeless design ensures its place as a cherished piece of radio history.
