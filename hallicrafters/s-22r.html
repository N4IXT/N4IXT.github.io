Overview

The Hallicrafters S-22R, also known as the Skyrider Marine, was a commercial receiver designed for maritime use. It was built to operate on both AC and DC power, making it suitable for shipboard service where 110 VDC power was common.

Technical Specifications

  • Number of Tubes: 8
  • Tube Types: 6SK7, 6K8, 6SK7, 6SK7, 6SQ7, 25L6, 6J5, 25Z5
  • Main Principle: Superheterodyne with RF-stage; IF frequency 1600 kHz
  • Tuned Circuits: 9 AM circuits
  • Wave Bands: Broadcast, Long Wave, and 2 x Short Wave
  • Frequency Coverage: 0.110-0.410 MHz, 0.400-1.5 MHz, 1.7-5.9 MHz, 5.3-18 MHz (with a reception gap from 1500-1700 kHz)
  • Power Type and Voltage: AC/DC, 117 Volt
  • Loudspeaker: Permanent Magnet Dynamic (PDyn), 5-inch diameter
  • Power Output: 2.5 W
  • Material: Metal case
  • Dimensions: 18.5 x 8.5 x 9.38 inches (470 x 216 x 238 mm)
  • Weight: 26 lbs 1.6 oz (11.850 kg)

Notable Features

The S-22R was designed with maritime needs in mind, featuring a frequency range that covered essential maritime communication bands. Its robust construction and ability to operate on both AC and DC power made it a reliable choice for shipboard use.

Historical Context

The Hallicrafters S-22R was produced during a time when maritime communication was crucial, especially during World War II. Its design reflects the technological advancements and practical needs of that era.
