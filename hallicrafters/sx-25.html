Overview

The Hallicrafters SX-25, also known as the Super Defiant, is a vintage communications receiver designed for amateur radio enthusiasts. It was produced during the early 1940s and became well-regarded for its performance and reliability.

  • Power Output: 8 W
  • Material: Metal case
  • Dimensions: 19.5 x 9.5 x 11.1 inches (495 x 241 x 283 mm)
  • Weight: 46 lbs 4.1 oz (21 kg)

Notable Features

The SX-25 was designed with a wide frequency range, covering essential communication bands. It featured a crystal IF filter and could operate on both AC and DC power, making it versatile for various settings.

Historical Context

The Hallicrafters SX-25 was produced during World War II, a period when reliable communication equipment was crucial. Its robust design and performance made it a popular choice among amateur radio operators and professionals alike.
