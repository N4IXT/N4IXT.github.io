Hallicrafters S-94 Civic Patrol Receiver

Years of Production: 1955-1959

The Hallicrafters S-94 Civic Patrol Receiver was a VHF receiver designed for monitoring public service bands, including police, fire, and other emergency services. It was a popular choice for hobbyists and professionals alike during its production years.

Technical Specifications:

  • Frequency Range: 152-174 MHz
  • Mode: AM
  • Tubes: 6-tube superheterodyne design
  • Power Supply: 120V AC
  • Speaker: Built-in 4-inch speaker

Notable Features:

  • Compact and lightweight design
  • Simple tuning mechanism for easy operation
  • High sensitivity for reliable reception
  • Durable metal chassis

Historical Context:

The Hallicrafters S-94 was introduced during a time when monitoring public service communications was becoming increasingly popular among radio enthusiasts. Its affordability and ease of use made it accessible to a wide audience. The S-94 is now considered a collectible item, valued for its historical significance and role in early VHF communication monitoring.
