Hallicrafters R-44 Speaker

Years of Production: 1947 - 1955

The Hallicrafters R-44 is a vintage external speaker designed to complement Hallicrafters' line of shortwave and amateur radio receivers. It is renowned for its robust build quality and excellent audio performance.

Technical Specifications:

  • Speaker Type: 8-inch dynamic speaker
  • Impedance: 3.2 ohms
  • Power Handling: 10 watts
  • Enclosure: Metal cabinet with a perforated grille
  • Dimensions: Approximately 10 x 10 x 6 inches

Notable Features:

  • Designed to match the aesthetic of Hallicrafters receivers, such as the SX-42 and SX-43.
  • Provides clear and rich audio output for enhanced listening experience.
  • Durable construction suitable for long-term use.

Historical Context:

The R-44 speaker was introduced during the post-World War II era, a time when amateur radio was experiencing a surge in popularity. Hallicrafters, a leading manufacturer of radio equipment, designed the R-44 to meet the needs of enthusiasts who required high-quality external speakers for their setups. Its stylish design and reliable performance made it a popular choice among radio operators of the time.

Additional Details:

The R-44 is still sought after by collectors and vintage radio enthusiasts today. Its timeless design and compatibility with classic Hallicrafters receivers make it a prized addition to any vintage radio collection.
