Hallicrafters SX-110

Years of Production: 1959-1965

The Hallicrafters SX-110 is a communications receiver designed for amateur radio enthusiasts. It covers a wide frequency range and offers several advanced features for its time.

Technical Specifications:

  • Frequency Range: 0.538-1.6, 1.55-4.6, 4.6-13, 12-34 MHz
  • Modulation: AM
  • Receiver System: Superheterodyne
  • IF Frequency: 455 kHz
  • Audio Output: 2 W
  • Power Requirements: 105-125 Volt AC
  • Number of Tubes: 8
  • Wave Bands: Broadcast and multiple shortwave bands
  • Price in First Year of Sale: $160.00

Notable Features:

  • Superheterodyne principle with a crystal filter for improved selectivity
  • Permanent Magnet Dynamic (PDyn) Loudspeaker for enhanced audio quality
  • Metal case for durability
  • S-Meter, Antenna Trimmer, and Crystal Filter for better performance

Historical Context:

The Hallicrafters SX-110 was produced during a period when amateur radio was experiencing significant growth. Hallicrafters was renowned for its innovative designs and high-quality craftsmanship, making the SX-110 a popular choice among radio enthusiasts. Its robust construction and reliable performance contributed to its reputation as a dependable communications receiver.
