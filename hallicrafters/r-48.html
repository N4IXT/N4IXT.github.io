Hallicrafters R-48 Speaker

Years of Production: 1950s

The Hallicrafters R-48 is a vintage external speaker designed to complement Hallicrafters shortwave and amateur radio receivers. It was a popular accessory during the mid-20th century, known for its robust build and audio clarity.

Technical Specifications:

  • Speaker Type: 8-inch dynamic speaker
  • Impedance: 3.2 ohms
  • Frequency Response: Optimized for voice and communication signals
  • Enclosure: Wooden cabinet with a metal grille

Notable Features:

  • Designed to match the aesthetic of Hallicrafters receivers
  • Durable construction for long-term use
  • Improved audio quality for clearer reception of signals

Historical Context:

The R-48 speaker was introduced during the golden age of shortwave radio, when Hallicrafters was a leading manufacturer of amateur radio equipment. It was often paired with popular receivers like the SX-99 and SX-100, providing enhanced audio performance for radio enthusiasts and operators.

Additional Details:

The Hallicrafters R-48 remains a sought-after item among collectors of vintage radio equipment. Its classic design and historical significance make it a prized addition to any collection.
