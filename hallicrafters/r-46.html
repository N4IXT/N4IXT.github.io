Overview

The Hallicrafters R-46 is a vintage tabletop speaker designed to complement various Hallicrafters receivers. It was produced in the early 1950s and became known for its robust construction and reliable performance.

Technical Specifications

  • Type: Permanent Magnet Dynamic (PDyn) Loudspeaker
  • Speaker Diameter: 10 inches (25.4 cm)
  • Power Type: No power needed
  • Material: Metal case
  • Dimensions: 15 x 11 x 11 inches (381 x 279 x 279 mm)
  • Weight: 17 lbs (7.7 kg)
  • Matching Transformer: 5 k ohm
  • Audio Range: 80 Hz to 8,000 Hz

Notable Features

The R-46 speaker was designed to match Hallicrafters receivers such as the SX-62, SX-71, SX-73, and SX-88. It features a 5 k ohm matching transformer and a wide audio range, making it suitable for high-fidelity audio reproduction.

Historical Context

The Hallicrafters R-46 was produced during a time when high-quality audio equipment was becoming increasingly important for both amateur and professional radio operators. Its design and performance made it a popular choice for enhancing the audio experience of Hallicrafters receivers.
