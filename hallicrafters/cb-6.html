Hallicrafters CB-6 Radio

Years of Production: 1959-1962

The Hallicrafters CB-6 was a citizen's band transceiver designed for short-range communication. It was a popular choice among amateur radio enthusiasts during its production years.

Technical Specifications

  • Frequency Range: 27 MHz (Citizen's Band)
  • Power Output: 5 Watts
  • Mode: AM (Amplitude Modulation)
  • Power Supply: 110V AC or 12V DC (with optional adapter)
  • Dimensions: 10 x 7 x 5 inches

Notable Features

  • Compact and portable design
  • Built-in speaker and microphone
  • Simple and intuitive controls
  • Durable metal chassis

Historical Context

The Hallicrafters CB-6 was introduced during the early days of citizen's band radio, a time when CB communication was gaining popularity for personal and business use. It was one of the early models that helped establish CB radio as a practical and accessible communication tool for the general public.

Additional Details

The CB-6 is now considered a collector's item among vintage radio enthusiasts. Its robust construction and classic design make it a sought-after piece for those interested in the history of radio communication.
