Hallicrafters CB-34

Years of Production: 1960s

The Hallicrafters CB-34 is a vintage citizens band (CB) radio that was popular during the 1960s. It was designed for short-distance radio communication and was widely used by hobbyists and professionals alike.

Technical Specifications:

  • Frequency Range: 27 MHz (CB Band)
  • Channels: 23 channels
  • Power Output: 5 watts (AM)
  • Modulation: Amplitude Modulation (AM)
  • Power Supply: 110V AC or 12V DC (with optional adapter)

Notable Features:

  • Classic tube-based design for superior audio quality
  • Front-panel microphone input
  • Large, easy-to-read channel selector
  • Durable metal chassis

Historical Context:

The Hallicrafters CB-34 was produced during the golden age of CB radio, a time when these devices were gaining popularity for personal and business communication. The CB-34 was known for its reliability and high-quality construction, making it a favorite among radio enthusiasts.

Additional Details:

Hallicrafters was a renowned manufacturer of amateur and commercial radio equipment, and the CB-34 is a testament to their commitment to quality and innovation. Today, the CB-34 is considered a collector's item and is sought after by vintage radio enthusiasts.
