Hallicrafters R-47 Speaker

Years of Production: 1947-1950

The Hallicrafters R-47 is a vintage external speaker designed to complement Hallicrafters shortwave and amateur radio receivers. It was introduced in the late 1940s and became a popular accessory for radio enthusiasts of the era.

Technical Specifications:

  • Speaker Type: 8-inch permanent magnet dynamic speaker
  • Impedance: 3.2 ohms
  • Power Handling: 10 watts
  • Cabinet Material: Metal with a painted finish
  • Dimensions: Approximately 10 x 10 x 6 inches

Notable Features:

  • Designed to match the aesthetic of Hallicrafters receivers, such as the SX-42 and SX-43.
  • Durable metal construction with a stylish grille design.
  • Provides enhanced audio quality for communications and shortwave listening.

Historical Context:

The R-47 speaker was introduced during the post-World War II era, a time when amateur radio and shortwave listening were gaining popularity. Hallicrafters was a leading manufacturer of radio equipment, and the R-47 was marketed as a high-quality accessory to improve the listening experience. Its robust design and compatibility with various Hallicrafters receivers made it a sought-after item among radio enthusiasts.

Additional Details:

The Hallicrafters R-47 is now considered a collector's item, prized for its historical significance and its role in the golden age of amateur radio. Restored units are often displayed alongside vintage Hallicrafters receivers in collections and museums.
